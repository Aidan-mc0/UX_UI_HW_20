I am a UX/UI Designer based in Toronto.

My UX Skills

User Research

Every good design solution relies on a strong foundation of good research. The research helps frame exactly what problem I need to solve. The insights I gather from my user research helps to inform my decisions every step of the way during my design process. In order to solve a problem you first have to understand exactly what it is that a user wants and then I can address those problems.

User Interface Design

If the interface is the way users interact with your website or mobile app, then it is increasingly important for me as a UI designer to design intuitive and engaging user interfaces. By extension, that means within the user interface, I always aim to craft a memorable user experience that positively represents the clients I design for.

Web Development

It's one thing to talk, but is it really impressive if you can put your money where your mouth is? Coding isn't for everyone, however having CSS/HTML/JavaScript knowledge ensures freedom in my design process. Front-end development is what brings each project from just a good idea or a problem you want to solve to reality; a design that solves problems.

Greenhorn

NCC Web Redesign

A proposed modernized redesign of Nature Conservancy Canada's website to help meet their 2030 strategic goals. NCC going into 2030 wants to target mobile users in order to meet their supporter goals, and therefore need a responsive web design that is optimized for mobile screens.
